To do After VDS

  • Deposit to govt. treasury within 7th of next following month;

  • Issue certificate (Mushok 6.6) to the service provider within 3 days after deposit to treasury;

  • Show the deducted amount of VAT in your VAT return under note 24 and

  • Include the treasury challan under note 58 of your VAT Return.

VAT Law Ref.

To do After TDS

  • Deposit to the govt. treasury within 02 weeks of the next following month (except for June);

  • Issue certificate to beneficiary well in advance before he submits his annual tax return.

Tax Law Ref.
